Neelum Jhelum

The Project diverts Neelum river water through tunnels at Nauseri about 41 km upstream of Muzaffarabad and out falling in Jhelum River near Chatter Kalas in AJ&K, where the powerhouse is located. With an installed capacity of 969 MW, the dam produces annual energy of 5150 GWh and was completed in 2018.
